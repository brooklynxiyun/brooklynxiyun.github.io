Chocolate lava cake recipe


Home Email Us

ingredients:

  • 125g dark chocolate chopped
  • 125g unsalted butter
  • 3 large eggs
  • 35g plain flour

    • recipe:

    • Makes 6 chocolate puddings
      •
    • 1. Melt chocolate and butter.
    • 2. In another bowl, whisk together eggs, sugar, flour until just blended.
    • 3. Gradually whisk in melted chocolate and butter.
    • 4. Grease and flour 250ml ramekins.
    • 5. Pour mixture into ramekins and bake at 200 degrees Celsius for 10-12 minutes, until the tops are firm and cracking slightly and the edges set.
    • 6. Serve immediately!
